West India

Baga Beach

Baga is a famous beach destination in North Goa that attracts thousands of tourists and watersports lovers to its heart. The beach shares its coastal line from the environs of Sinquerim, Candolim, and Baga, with the Baga shore being more lively and enticing.

Kutch

Virtually an island that resembles the shape of a tortoise, Kutch is an erstwhile princely state of India holding onto its grandeur nature from the past.

Gateway of India

Renowned as one of the most iconic structures of India, Gateway of India, was constructed along the Mumbai Harbour in 1924. Located at the tip of Apollo Bunder, this massive structure represents a commemorate memorial.

Ellora caves

Ajanta and Ellora caves complex is adorned with beautiful sculptures,and paintings,and frescoes and include Buddhist monasteries, Hindu and Jain temples.

Dudhani Lake

Amidst Silvassa’s beautiful landscape with small villages, winding rivers, gorgeous waterfalls and patches of tall green trees, stands the large and beautiful Dudhni lake.

Nagoa Beach

The beach is also a popular picnic spot and lies by the old fishing village of Nagoa.Most unique feature of this beach is, however and the shoe shape it bends into giving the beach a very unique aesthetic.

Elephant's Head Point

As the name suggests, Elephant’s Head Point is a mountain rock that has an uncanny resemblance to an elephant’s head. Easily one of the best places to visit in Mahabaleshwar in one day, Elephant’s Head Point offers a mesmerising view of the Sahyadri.

Statue of Unity

The Statue of Unity is a colossal statue of Indian statesman and independence activist Vallabhbhai Patel, who was the first Deputy Prime Minister and Home Minister of independent India and an adherent of Mahatma Gandhi during the nonviolent Indian Independence.

Gir National Park

Also known as Sasan Gir, this wildlife sanctuary is known the world over for not only being one of the few known habitats of the Asiatic lion but also for the efforts taken here to prevent this majestic species from getting extinct. This is the only place on the planet, besides Africa, where you can spot this species.
